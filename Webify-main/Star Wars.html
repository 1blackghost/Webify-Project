Preview

Star Wars: The Empire Strikes Back is the second installment in the Star Wars saga and is widely considered one of the greatest sequels of all time. Directed by Irvin Kershner and written by Leigh Brackett and Lawrence Kasdan, the movie continues the story of Luke Skywalker as he learns the ways of the Jedi from Master Yoda while also trying to rescue his friends from the clutches of the evil Darth Vader and the Galactic Empire. With its iconic moments, memorable characters, and a story that explores deeper themes of identity, love and sacrifice, the Empire Strikes Back is a cinematic masterpiece that solidified the Star Wars franchise as a cultural phenomenon.
